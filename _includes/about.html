About Me

My academic interests include Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I obtained my undergraduate degree from BITS Pilani, India, in May 2016.

Currently, I am working on my Graduate Specialization research involving relationship extraction methods in natural language processing, particularly applied to the area investigative journalism. In the recent past, I worked on topic modeling of self-disclosed textual information in online user profiles. Details about all my projects so far can be found on my LinkedIn profile.
