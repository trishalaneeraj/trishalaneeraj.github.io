About Me

Hi! My name is Trishala Neeraj. I am a first-year graduate student in the Information Science Department at Cornell University at Cornell Tech. My academic interests include Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I obtained my undergraduate degree from BITS Pilani, India, in May 2016.

Currently, I am working on my Graduate Specialization research involving relationship extraction methods in natural language processing, particularly applied to the area investigative journalism. In the recent past, I worked on topic modeling of self-disclosed textual information in online user profiles as part of research at the Social Technology Lab. In the past I have also been a part of the Predictive Technology Laboratory at University of Virginia, while I worked there as a visiting research intern, where I completed my Undergraduate Thesis under the supervision of Dr. Matthew Gerber. Details about all my projects so far can be found on my LinkedIn profile.
